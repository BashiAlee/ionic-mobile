Become a Contributor

Why are we asking for your help?

We believe that community leaders and professionals who have moral grounding are the best examples to impart life-changing wisdom and are exemplary role models who can have a positive impact on young people. With your help, we can empower our young people and give them a sense of self-worth and opportunity, increase their social competency, and improve their academic and professional prospects.

How can you help?

We are seeking your participation as a “Contributor” to get involved by contributing your experiences, knowledge, in the form of articles, blogs, Vlogs, video content, podcast, music, poems, image visuals, art, stories, history, etc to the collective. Your professional background, thought leadership, and expertise can play an important role in guiding the development of today’s young people into tomorrow’s leaders. With your help, let’s make a HUGE impact for our young people.

CREATING GREAT CONTRIBUTOR CONTENT GUIDE

Please use the guide below to help you create great contributor content.  Not all points outlined below are necessary for great content. However, content that follows most of the points described in the guide below will do better through our approval and publishing process and will have a lower chance of revision requests:

  • • Check your spelling and grammar thoroughly
  • • Aim to write content that is written on a particular theme
  • • Follow a story format (opening, body, close)
  • • Use a catchy title - something that would make you curious!
  • • Optimize your content to do well in searches
  • • Write content that excites you and that you are passionate about in such a way that it would excite and ignite passion in another
  • • Write about what is relevant in the news today or trending and why what is happening is important
  • • Write about your experiences and why that experience may be relevant or important to others
  • • Consider the age and readership styles of your audience
  • • Have others review your work and write often

HOW CONTENT IS APPROVED AND PUBLISHED

Content Review and Approval Cycle

Content we receive will not be published simultaneously - it will go through an approval process, editing, additional content integration, media integration, and prep for launch via our schedule.

Publishing Time-Sensitive Contributor Content - There are exceptions to publishing time-sensitive content.  

If an exception needs to be considered based on the time sensitivity of the content, the time-sensitive content will be published as soon as it goes through our approval and modification process through the Cliiimb Newsroom.  Examples of this are content that may be news, cover stories, key interviews, etc that cover a subject happening in real-time that are having real-time impact and requires the readers to perform an action as a result of the content that cannot be delayed.

CONTRIBUTOR CONTENT MODIFICATION PROCESS

Our objective with modifying contributor content is to enhance it so that the subjects discussed are brought to life in a way that is impactful and engaging to target audiences.

What Happens to Submitted Contributor Content Once it is Approved

All submitted contributor content that is approved will get a cover image.

Optional Modifications

Contributions may be collected together to tell a story regarding a particular topic, modified and expanded upon in order to enhance the content, or augmented with digital media such as images and YouTube videos (if any relevant videos are found).  If a contribution is exceptionally good, we may go out of our way to create custom digital media to enhance the content (e.g. we use our videographer and film team to do an interview with the author about the subject and add that finished media piece to the content).  

Cliiimb Newsroom

Cliiimb has established a support newsroom consisting of editors, writers, creative, copy researchers and proofer to enhance the delivery of Cliiimb contributions. The Cliiimb newsroom team may support the enhancement of a contributions by adding to the piece - additional content copy,images, video, audio that are relevant to the submitted content from contributors.  

How the Newsroom Will Evolve

As Cliiimb grows, we intend drive the development of custom story converge, documentary profile of our community leaders and young people by spotlighting them. Cliiimb also plans to network with schools nationally to engage journalism students through Internship, volunteers and supporters to work with Cliiimb newsroom. We will be constantly looking for additional ways to support the proficiency of the Cliiimb newsroom to drive high quality engaging content to showcase the Cliiimb Mission.
